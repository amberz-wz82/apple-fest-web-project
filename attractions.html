Apple & Cider Trail: Shops & Restaurants

For a better read of the image, click here.

Wants to see where each shop and restaurant is located in Downtown Ithaca? Check out the Fest Map to see their locations!

Celebrate the apple season this year. Enjoy a little bit of the season each day with your quaranteam crew on a socially distant, contactless, taste-tastic journey of sipping, noshing, & shopping.

Happening all week long inside shops and restaurants around downtown from Monday, Sept. 28 - Sunday, Oct. 4th. Enjoy ciders by the glass with your meal, juice for purchase, fun apple-inspired novelties, and even sweet and savory dishes showcasing the mighty fruit!

Guidelines how to shop in store will vary from in-store to online shopping. | Curbside pick up options may also be available with participating businesses.
